Welcome to the garden planning map!

Note the gardening zone for your area. Soil type information is also available once you zoom in to your location.

Read through the publication, Where to Put Your Vegetable Garden, for helpful tips on sunlight, trees, soil, and location as you plan your specific garden site.

Use the measurement tools to take intial measurements, then use the editor to place your garden boundaries, then sketch sections and select vegetable varieties for each section.

When you are settled on a garden design, click on the Export Report button to save a report detailing the yield you can expect from each garden section.

To save your work, be sure to log in with your ArcGIS Online account.
